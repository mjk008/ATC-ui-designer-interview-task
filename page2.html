User flow of the app

Startup

So basically the user must have a account to access the apps features. If the user has no account no problem he can create one

Booking a service

Ok once the user is in he/she can explore the list of available service in their home screen with different options then they can browse the service and confirm their pickup

Tracking

After their confirmation of the particular service they will see tracking screen where there will be deatails of whos coming to solve their problem with their contact and other details. When the service person is near the user can wave their phone for help.

Finishing up and payments

After their problem with the vehicle is solved the user can pay the service person through digital and cash payments.
