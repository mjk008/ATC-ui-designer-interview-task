Information Architecture Chart 

Yeah due to many actions and screens this app has a really complex architecture but i have simplified it as easy as possible

The app basically has the following main sections
  1. Authentication and account - so typically like any other app this app also has a dedicated accounts section for each inorder to keep in track of users activity.
  2. home and booking section - this is where all the main tasks happen where the user books and benefits from the services. the main part of this is the payments as this is more like a on field service the payments are received in different mediums, mainly as digital directly to the app, or by the service man as cash. 
  3. Settings - it is also a main part dealing with all customizations for an individual including, profile settings, privacy, and transactions monitoring, simply like a dashboard.
